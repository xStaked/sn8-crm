# Phase 02.1 Plan 02: Qualification Handoff and Draft Orchestration Summary

**Asynchronous AI-sales orchestration from `conversationId`, versioned Spanish sales prompts, and pending-review customer status messaging without quote delivery**

## Accomplishments

- Added a queue-safe `AiSalesOrchestrator` and `AiSalesProcessor` that accept a qualification-complete `conversationId`, load transcript history, upsert the commercial brief, and create a reviewable draft asynchronously.
- Introduced versioned prompt builders for premium consultive brief extraction and quote draft generation, with explicit separation between customer statements, agent inferences, and owner-review content.
- Added a bounded outbound status update that informs the customer their proposal is under internal review while keeping quote body, price breakdown, and commercial commitment out of customer-facing code paths.

## Decisions Made

- The qualification handoff contract stays loosely coupled as `conversationId` plus queue metadata, so Phase 2 can trigger AI-sales later without rewriting the orchestration boundary.
- AI draft payloads now preserve `clientStatedNeeds`, `agentInferences`, and owner-review content separately to keep later review loops auditable.
- Customer communication in this phase is limited to a review-status notification persisted as an outbound message with `source: ai-sales-pending-review`.
